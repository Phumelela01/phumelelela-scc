Early Intervention Projects

  • Specialised education and learning support
  • Daily meals (breakfast and lunch)
  • Life skills training and developmental activities
  • Disability awareness and inclusion advocacy

Our early intervention programme supports holistic development for children with disabilities in Ezibeleni. We provide a safe, caring environment focused on growth, independence, and dignity.

Skills Development

  • Gardening: Sustainable food production.
  • Baking: Teaching culinary basics and income generation.
  • Farming: Pig and poultry care.
  • Beadwork: Creativity through handmade crafts.
  • Jam & Juice Making: Food preservation techniques.
